AQUA - THE 24-HOUR COFFEE SHOP

Step into the refreshingly designed and aquatically-themed AQUA and coffee becomes a leisurely, wondrous affair. Or, choose from a selection of International, Indian and Asian flavours. If buffet is on the mind, the restaurant also serves buffet meals for breakfast and dinner. Here, surrounded by water bodies, you can’t but stare wistfully into the Arabian Sea.

HAAOCHI

Not just its sophisticated ambience, a decadent menu here helps some lip-smacking sessions. Contemporary Chinese cuisine, with a blend of different regional cuisines of China such as Xiang (Hunan), Chuan (Sichuan) and Guangdong (Cantonese) gently but assuredly blow away your mind. Add to that, an elaborate dim sum menu and a full-service bar.
